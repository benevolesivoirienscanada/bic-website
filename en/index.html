Who we are

A family united by a love for Côte d'Ivoire.

The Ivorian Volunteers of Canada (BIC) are a community of passionate and committed individuals, united by their love for Côte d'Ivoire and their desire to contribute positively to Canadian society. We work to build connections, organize cultural and social activities, and support the members of our community.
