Our story

The Bénévoles Ivoiriens du Canada (BIC) was born from the desire of a group of committed individuals to create a gathering space for the Ivorian community established in Canada. Facing the challenges of integration and the distance from home, BIC was founded to build strong bonds, preserve Ivorian culture and offer a solid support network to its members.

Since its creation, the organization has continued to grow, expanding its activities, its committees, and its reach within the Ivorian community in Montréal, Gatineau, and beyond.

Our mission

BIC's mission is to:

  • Bring together Ivorians and friends of Côte d'Ivoire established in Canada
  • Promote Ivorian culture, values and heritage
  • Facilitate the integration and personal growth of community members
  • Encourage solidarity and mutual support between volunteers
  • Contribute positively to Canadian society through charitable and social actions
Our values

What unites us.

Solidarity

We celebrate our Ivorian heritage while thriving in our host country.

We welcome everyone with warmth, without distinction, in a spirit of openness and respect.

Commitment

Our volunteers give their time and energy to move our shared cause forward.
