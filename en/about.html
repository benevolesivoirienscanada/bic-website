Our story

The Bénévoles Ivoiriens du Canada (BIC) was born from the desire of a group of committed individuals to create a gathering space for the Ivorian community established in Canada. Facing the challenges of integration and the distance from home, BIC was founded to build strong bonds, preserve Ivorian culture and offer a solid support network to its members.

Since its creation, the organization has continued to grow, expanding its activities, its committees, and its reach within the Ivorian community in Montréal, Gatineau, and beyond.

Our mission

BIC's mission is to:

  • Mobilize resources on a small scale to enhance our cultural and educational knowledge
  • Promote the resources of our country
  • Improve the living conditions of vulnerable people

Our vision

BIC's vision is to:

  • Be a channel for mobilizing the Ivorian diaspora abroad through impactful projects in Côte d'Ivoire and Canada that inspire awareness among youth
Our values

What unites us.

Human

At the heart of everything we do, people. We welcome, listen and support each member of our community with respect and warmth.

Rigour

We carry out our commitments with seriousness and transparency. Every donation, every volunteer hour, every promise matters — and deserves to be kept.

Cooperation

Alone, we go fast; together, we go far. We believe in the power of the collective: volunteers, partners and community move forward hand in hand.
