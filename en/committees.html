Our committees

Five committees, one shared vision.

BIC runs thanks to volunteers organized into five committees, each playing an essential role in the life of the organization.

📣
Committee 1

Communication

The Communication committee is BIC's voice. It manages the organization's online presence, creates engaging content and promotes activities to the community.

Responsibilities
  • Social media management (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
  • Design of posters and promotional visuals
  • Weekly content publishing: Ivorian proverbs, news, updates from Côte d'Ivoire and Canada
  • Live coverage of BIC events

Committee 2

Activities

The Activities committee is the engine behind BIC's events. It designs and organizes cultural, social and sports activities to bring the community together and create memorable moments.

A few past activities
  • Screening of the film GLA with actress Ahissa Be
  • Hiking trips in Gatineau and Montréal
  • Kizomba × Rétro Ivoire Night — dance class and social evening
  • Coverage of AFCON 2025
Among the upcoming activities
  • Online panel discussion — June 2026
  • Ivorian Village IV — July–August 2026
  • Hiking trips — September 2026
  • Arts gala — October–November 2026 (to be confirmed)
🤲
Committee 3

Chill'ons et Offrons

Born from the merger of the Integration committee and the Charity committee, Chill'ons et Offrons combines conviviality and generosity. It organizes gatherings and charitable actions for people in need.

Achievements 2025–2026
  • Friendly gatherings in Gatineau and Montréal
  • Preparation of 250+ sandwiches given to Bread & Beyond for homeless people in Montréal
📊

Committee 5

Strategic Development

The Strategic Development committee thinks about BIC's future. It identifies funding sources, structures long-term projects and ensures the sustainable growth of the organization.

Focus areas
  • Development of sustainable revenue (sponsorships, grants, flagship activities)
  • Strategic planning of projects in three categories: flagship, creative, and social impact
  • Projects in development: Ivorian Village IV, Newcomer's Handbook, support forms
